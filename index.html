All-day dynamic monitoring

Curve3 Ultra features all-day dynamic monitoring to continuously track heart rate, blood oxygen, and stress—keeping you informed about your health anytime, anywhere.

Customizable Buttons

Easily assign your favorite functions—like workout tracking, stopwatch, flashlight, or SOS—to physical buttons for faster, more intuitive control.

ZN-27 All zinc alloy bezel

Curve3 is crafted from aerospace-grade ZN27 zinc alloy, undergoing a highly complex 17-step process to deliver exceptional metal strength. Paired with a finely brushed metal finish, the frame offers a delicate yet premium texture.
